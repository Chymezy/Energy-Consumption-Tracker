Our Mission

Our mission with eTracker is to empower businesses to achieve significant energy savings, reduce operational costs, and minimize their carbon footprint. Through real-time monitoring and advanced analytics, we aim to provide actionable insights that drive sustainable energy practices.

What is eTracker?

Innovative Energy Management Solution

eTracker is a comprehensive web application developed by EG Solutions to address the critical challenges of energy consumption in the industrial and commercial sectors. With features like detailed energy consumption tracking, trend analysis, and personalized insights, eTracker enables users to make informed decisions about their energy use.

Key Features

User Registration and Login

  • Secure Access: Users can create accounts and log in to access personalized energy consumption data.
  • Personalized Experience: Ensures data privacy and a tailored user experience.

Adding Energy Consumption Data

  • Easy Data Entry: Users can input and save their daily energy usage data.
  • Accurate Tracking: Enables precise monitoring of energy consumption over time.

Viewing Energy Consumption Trends

  • Dynamic Charts: Users can view energy consumption trends and patterns through interactive charts.
  • Insightful Analysis: Helps identify inefficiencies and optimize energy usage.

Updating Energy Consumption Data

  • Data Management: Users can update or delete previous energy consumption entries.
  • Reliable Records: Ensures data accuracy and reliability.

Average Energy Consumption Insights

  • Performance Insights: Users can view average energy consumption over specified periods.
  • Informed Decisions: Provides insights into overall energy performance and areas for improvement.
